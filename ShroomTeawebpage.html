Nunu's Shroom Tea Recipe

    Back

Ingredients and Supplies

  • Psilocybin Mushrooms
  • A Scale That Can Measure Grams
  • Lemon Juice (preferably fresh)
  • One Herbal Tea Bag
  • Hot Water
  • 2 Coffee Mugs
  • Mesh Strainer or Cheese Cloth, (when using a cheese cloth be sure the fabric is not woven too tightly)
  • Small Plate to Cover Tea
  • An Empty Sink
  • A Sense of Openness to the Universe

Instructions

  1. Give thanks to the mushrooms and the universe that created them.
  2. Take one mushroom and cut off 1/4 of a piece
  3. Measure 0.5 grams of mushroom
  4. Cut mushroom into small pieces and put in empty mug
  5. Squeeze half a lemon or pour 1 teaspoon lemon juice over the mushrooms
  6. Place small plate over mug and let rest for 15 minutes
  7. Boil water and pour into mug with lemon and mushroom mixture
  8. Place second mug in an empty sink
  9. Cover second mug with a mesh strainer or cheese cloth
  10. Pour the contents of the first mug into the second mug and strain out as much plant matter as possible
  11. Place tea bag into second mug, cover and let rest for 5 minutes
  12. Enjoy your tea
  • Drink one cup of shroom tea every three days.
